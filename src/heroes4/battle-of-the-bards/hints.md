# Hints (bottle messages)

The message in the bottle reads, "Thank you for picking up litter!"

A level 3 (hero class) can easily defeat a few (level 2 units).

When you defeat a hostile force, the XP is divided evenly among the heroes in the victorious army.

Specializing in Death magic and Nature magic will make your hero a Demonologist, with a +50% bonus to all demon-summoning spells.

Specializing in Order magic and Nature magic will make your hero an Enchanter, with a 20% bonus to all summoning and illusion spells.

Specializing in Scouting and Tactics will make your hero a Field Marshal. Creatures in a Field Marshal's army get a 10% attack bonus.

Specializing in Scouting and Chaos Magic will make your hero a Fire Diviner with a 20% bonus to all fire-based spells.

Specializing in Scouting and Order Magic will make your hero a Seer and give you +2 scouting radius.

Have you considered the advantages inherent in owning a town?
* More creatures for your army
* More spells for your heroes
* Temporary buffs when you visit
* Throw your enemies in prison

Town Gate is an extremely useful spell, letting you teleport instantly to your town, and even escape from combat with your army intact. But it cannot teleport you to any town whose entrance is closer than 10 tiles away. Thus, if you have two towns, you can use Town Gate to teleport between them.

Specializing in Life magic and Nature magic will make your hero a Summoner, and give you a 20% bonus to the Summoning skill.

Specializing in Chaos magic and Nature magic will make your hero a Warlock, giving you 10 extra spell points and +1 spell point regen per day.
