## Audio, pre-1923

Under the Music Modernization Act, sound recordings prior to 1923 entered the
public domain January 1st, 2022. ([Wikipedia][mma])

- [Recordings by Antonio Pozo, "El Mochuelo"](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=OLAK5uy_mhXZoP1QBpgtOS3DfCznmtUztoty7EQeA).
  Exact date unknown, but [almost certainly before 1913](https://adp.library.ucsb.edu/index.php/mastertalent/detail/208123/Pozo_Antonio_El_Mochuelo). See also [this compilation](https://benchristel.github.io/yt/#5j8DDDjXXMM). [El Mochuelo on Wikimedia](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Antonio_Pozo_%22El_Mochuelo%22)
- [Cielito Lindo - Trío González](https://benchristel.github.io/yt/#kHrI5EUAEBM) (1920)
- [Crazy Blues - Mamie Smith](https://benchristel.github.io/yt/#qaz4Ziw_CfQ) (1920)
- [Kamarinskaya](https://benchristel.github.io/yt/#fTRleoA1EW8) - Anton Ruzhek and the Life Guards Cavalry Regiment (1901)
- [La donna è mobile - Enrico Caruso](https://benchristel.github.io/yt/#https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3QnGTVZH4as) (1903, 1904, 1908)
- [Ave Maria - Alessandro Moreschi](https://benchristel.github.io/yt/#KLjvfqnD0ws) (1904)
- [Alvorada das rosas - Patápio Silva](https://benchristel.github.io/yt/#OFVypkhj9hI) (1904)
