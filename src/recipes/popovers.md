# Popovers

## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup white flour
- 3/4 cup milk
- 3 large eggs
- dash nutmeg
- 4 Tbsp butter
- lemon and confectioner's sugar for topping

## Procedure

1. Preheat oven to 425° F
2. Preheat 11–12" cast iron or heavy skillet (rounded sides encourage better edge popover)
3. Beat together: flour, milk, nutmeg, eggs
4. Melt butter in preheated skillet, swirl to distribute to edges/sides of skillet.
5. Pour batter into skillet distributing evenly.
6. Bake 14-16 min (or until edge pop and begin to brown)
7. Remove from oven and sprinkle with confectioner's sugar and a squeeze of
   lemon juice at the table.
