# Hummus

## Hardware

- Food processor

## Ingredients

- 1 lb dry garbanzo beans
- 1/2 cup olive oil
- 3 Tbsp tahini
- juice of 1 lemon
- 1 1/2 tsp salt

## Procedure

### Day 1

1. Soak the garbanzo beans overnight

### Day 2

1. Drain the beans and bring to a boil in fresh water.
2. Simmer 60-90 minutes, until tender. Optionally, add salt when the beans start to softenâ€”this can speed up the cooking.
3. Drain the beans and let them cool before making hummus.

### Hummus

1. Add all ingredients to a food processor. Blend until smooth.
