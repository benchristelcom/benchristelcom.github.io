# Hummingbird Food

This is for birds, not people!

## Ingredients

- 10 oz filtered water
- 5 Tbsp white granulated cane sugar

(4:1 water:sugar ratio)

Do not use brown sugar, unrefined/unbleached sugar, honey, or any other kind of sweetener. These are all bad for hummingbirds.

Unrefined sugar in particular can be high in iron, which is bad.

## Procedure

1. Boil water and mix with sugar in a heatproof vessel.
2. While the mixture cools, clean feeder with vinegar. Rinse well.
3. Pour food into feeder.
