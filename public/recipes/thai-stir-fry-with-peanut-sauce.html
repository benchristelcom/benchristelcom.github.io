Thai Stir-Fry with Peanut Sauce

Serves 10

Ingredients

Peanut Sauce

  • 1/2 cup smooth peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 Tbsp grated ginger
  • 1 tsp salt

Stir-fry

  • Vegetable oil
  • 1 lb firm tofu
  • 3 Tbsp toasted sesame oil
  • 6 scallions, minced
  • 2 Tbsp grated ginger
  • 2 Tbsp minced garlic
  • 4 carrots, thinly sliced
  • 2 large red bell peppers, diced
  • 1 lb asparagus, tough stems removed, chopped into 2-inch pieces
  • 1/2 lb snow peas
  • Thai basil, cilantro, or mint

Procedure

  1. Make the sauce by pureeing the ingredients together.
  2. Bake or air-fry the tofu. Toss with 1 cup of peanut sauce and set aside.
  3. Saut√© the white parts of the scallions, garlic, and ginger in 3 T sesame oil with 1/2 tsp salt on high heat.
  4. After 1 minute, add carrots, peppers, scallion tops, 1/2 tsp salt.
  5. After 2 minutes, add asparagus, 1/4 tsp salt, and a bit of water. Cook until the asparagus is a vibrant green and just tender.
  6. Add snow peas and cook 1 or 2 minutes more.
  7. Add herbs, tofu, and the remaining peanut sauce and cook until the sauce is warmed through.
