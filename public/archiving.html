Web Archives and Archiving Tools

Archives

  • archive.org - Probably needs no introduction. The "Wayback Machine" has snapshots of websites going back 3 decades.
  • Ghost Archive - Another archive. Currently, their collection is kind of sparse.

Archiving Tools

  • wayback-machine-downloader - Archives websites from the Wayback Machine (web.archive.org). Has some bugs and performance issues. I'm working on fixing those.
  • yt-dlp - Command-line video/audio downloader for YouTube
  • cobalt - Downloads media from many different sites
